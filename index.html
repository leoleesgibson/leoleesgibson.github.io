President Boakai Nominates and Appoints Additional Officials To Government

The President of the Republic of Liberia, His Excellency Joseph Nyuma Boakai, Sr., has nominated and appointed additional members of government affecting the Local Government and Office of the President.

President Boakai Praises Liberia's Surfing Potential

H.E President Joseph Nyuma Boakai, Sr., has expressed excitement over Liberia’s rise to a place among the top 10 surfing destinations in the world and the choice to host the Africa Tour: Surf to Rise competition.

President Boakai Condemns Recent Israel and Iran Conflict, Calls for Restoration of Peace.

President Joseph Nyuma Boakai, Sr., has condemned the recent escalation of violent conflict between Israel and Iran and finds it deeply regretful that political and diplomatic efforts have yet to bring peace and stability to the Middle East.

President Boakai Resigns from JNB Foundation Board as Chairman, Announces Successor

President Joseph Nyuma Boakai, Sr., has resigned his position as Chairman of the Joseph N. Boakai Foundation. In a letter addressed to his fellow board members, he cited his responsibilities as President of Liberia and the need to focus on national and international affairs as the reason for his resignation.

President Boakai Nominates and Appoints Additional Officials To Government

The President of the Republic of Liberia, His Excellency Joseph Nyuma Boakai, Sr., has nominated and appointed additional members of government affecting the Liberia Medical Health Regulatory Authority (LMHRA), the Liberia Institute for Public Administration (LIPA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), the National AIDS Commission, the Liberia Broadcasting System (LBS), the National Port Authority (NPA), the Ministry of Health (MOH), the Independent Information Commission (IIC), the National Fisheries and Aquaculture Authority, ((NaFAA), and the John F. Kennedy Medical Center (JFK).
